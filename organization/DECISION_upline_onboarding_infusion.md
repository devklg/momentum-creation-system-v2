## What happened

Kevin directed analysis of the upline's onboarding hub (rlegacymakers.com, Legacy Makers team) to identify elements worth infusing into MCS v2. The analysis produced ACR-0011 and a captured document inventory. This record memorializes the infusion decision and its locked parameters.

## What was infused

The upline's **5 Point Recruiting System** (List → Connect/Invite → Present → Follow Up → Onboard) and its launch milestones (five steps in 48h; QBA in 72h — one enrollment left leg + one right leg; CORE 3 on the third enrollment) are adopted as a tracked recruiting cycle inside MCS v2's ratified structures — not as a parallel system.

Mapping: prospect-facing steps 1–4 live in the **PMV** (prospect journey Identified → Enrolled; PMV owns awareness, CRM owns notes and follow-up tasks, Ivory owns invitation wording); the cycle rides Launch Center Stages 5–7; step 5 hands the new BA into Launch Stage 0 (duplication loop). Steve initializes the cycle from Discovery; Michael coaches it daily.

## Parameters locked by Kevin (2026-07-07)

1. Names list: **100 names**, built in **tranches of 20** — stored as PMV prospect records at stage Identified.
2. Prospect integration: **PMV + CRM only**. The lead qualification system (magnificent-lead-qualification-system-v1) is explicitly excluded.
3. Stall threshold: no qualifying launch activity for **24h inside the 72h QBA window, 72h thereafter**; sweep flags, Michael responds with why-replay.
4. QBA verification: **manual sponsor attestation** in the team/admin UI (writes milestone, activity, and Neo4j enrollment edges). No THREE back-office integration.
5. ACR number: **ACR-0011**, registered as Proposed, target v1.2.

The Wix link-farm delivery pattern (stateless, data leaks to third parties), PDF-as-training (Michael replaces it), and THREE corporate content duplication (link/ingest upstream, don't rebuild).
