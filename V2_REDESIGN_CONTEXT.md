# Momentum Creation System v2 Redesign Context

This repo is a separated v2 redesign workspace for UX and styling work.

`momentum-creation-system-v1` remains the stable completed version. Do not make
redesign experiments in v1.

## v2 Focus

1. Prospect Position & Momentum Center
2. Team Launch Center
3. Ivory Invitation Agent
4. Prospect Momentum Viewer
5. Team Magnificent brand/motion system

## Boundaries

- Do not rebuild backend logic unless required.
- Preserve existing business logic, package scripts, project structure, apps,
  server, shared packages, assets, and docs.
- Preserve token placement, CRM ownership, invitation spine, and compliance
  boundaries.
- Keep Team Magnificent / THREE compliance intact:
  - No AI lead qualification.
  - No automated prospecting or AI calling.
  - No income guarantees or spillover promises.
  - No CV amounts, cycle math, or dollar amounts on prospect pages.
  - Use PMV language only on prospect pages: People -> Momentum -> Volume -> Checks.
  - Alerts should say "[BA name] will reach out", never "Kevin will reach out".
