When it comes to skincare, the order in which you apply your products can make all the difference. Why Proper Layering Matters

Did you know that applying your skincare in the wrong order could reduce its effectiveness? According to dermatologists, layering your products correctly ensures that active ingredients are absorbed properly and not blocked by heavier products. The general rule of thumb is to start with the lightest product and finish with the heaviest. This way, each step prepares your skin for the next, creating a foundation for optimal results.
