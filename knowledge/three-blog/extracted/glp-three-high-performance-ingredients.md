Saffron Extract

This ancient botanical is making a strong comeback in the modern wellness landscape. Used for centuries, it is gaining momentum for its role in appetite and mood support. Its key components, safranal and crocin, are believed to help support feelings of fullness while also promoting a sense of well-being. Panax Ginseng Extract

Known for its adaptogenic properties (a substance’s ability to help the body adapt to physical and mental stress while maintaining homeostasis), panax ginseng extract liquid can help the body adapt to everyday stress while supporting healthy metabolic function. Its active compounds, called ginsenosides, are associated with maintaining homeostasis–your body’s ability to keep key systems like energy, metabolism, and stress response running within a healthy range. Ginsenosides, like those contained in Panax ginseng, may help your body maintain homeostasis while under stress, rather than letting stress throw energy and metabolic functions off balance. These benefits make Panax ginseng a valuable addition to any metabolic-focused formula like GLP THREETM.

Bitter Acids From Hops

Derived from hops, bitter acids can help stimulate the digestive system. Hops are flowers of the plant Humulus lupulus, a vine in the hemp family. Hops have been used for centuries–most commonly in beer, giving it its distinctive bitterness and aroma–but they also have a long history in herbal wellness. Bitter acids signal the body to start metabolic processes like enzyme and bile production, which directly impact how food is broken down and nutrients are used. Think of bitter acids like a kickstarter for your digestive system to support overall healthy and efficient gut function.
