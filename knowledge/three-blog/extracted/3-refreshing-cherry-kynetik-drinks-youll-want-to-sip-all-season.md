Cherry Ginger Spritz

A bubbly, slightly spicy refresher that balances sweet cherry with the zing of ginger.

Ingredients

-
1 packet Cherry Rush Kynetik

-
½ cup (4 oz) cold water

-
½ cup (4 oz) ginger beer

-
¼ cup fresh smashed cherries

-
Ice to serve

Instructions

-
In a glass, stir together Cherry Rush Kynetik and cold water until dissolved.

-
Add ginger beer and gently mix.

-
Stir in fresh smashed cherries.

-
Fill with ice, give it one last stir, and enjoy your spritz!

Frozen Cherry Mojito Mocktail

A mocktail that feels like a vacation—cool, minty, and bright with lime.

Ingredients

-
1 packet Cherry Rush Kynetik

-
½ cup cold water

-
½ cup soda water

-
Juice of ½ lime

-
Handful of mint leaves

-
Ice and cherries for garnish

Instructions

-
Add Cherry Rush Kynetik, cold water, lime juice, and mint leaves to a blender with ice.

-
Blend until slushy and smooth.

-
Pour into a glass, top with soda water, and give it a quick stir.

-
Garnish with extra cherries and mint. Sip and pretend you’re poolside.

Cherry Lime Pop

Simple, tangy, and naturally sweetened—perfect for an afternoon pick-me-up.

Ingredients

-
1 packet Cherry Rush Kynetik

-
8 oz cold water

-
1 oz fresh lime juice (about 1 lime)

-
1 tsp agave or honey

-
Lime slice and ice to garnish

Instructions

-
Mix Cherry Rush Kynetik with cold water until fully dissolved.

-
Add fresh lime juice and agave (or honey). Stir well.

-
Pour over ice and garnish with a lime slice for that extra pop.
