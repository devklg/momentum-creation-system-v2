What Is Bakuchiol?

Bakuchiol (pronounced buh-KOO-chee-all) is derived from the seeds and leaves of the Psoralea corylifolia plant, commonly known as the "babchi" plant. This ingredient has been used for centuries in traditional Ayurvedic and Chinese medicine, where it was prized for its calming and restorative properties. Today, Bakuchiol is making waves in the skincare world for its gentle yet effective results.

What Makes Bakuchiol Unique

- Derived from Nature: Extracted from the Psoralea corylifolia plant, bakuchiol is a botanical powerhouse.

- Universal Appeal: Suitable for all skin types, including sensitive or reactive skin.

- Sustainably Sourced: Bakuchiol aligns with THREE’s mission to combine the best of nature with cutting-edge science.

The Benefits of Bakuchiol

- Supports Collagen ProductionCollagen is the building block of youthful-looking skin, but its production naturally decreases over time. Bakuchiol works to support the skin’s natural collagen processes, helping to maintain elasticity and firmness.

- Provides Antioxidant SupportEnvironmental factors like UV rays and pollution can cause oxidative stress on the skin. Bakuchiol’s antioxidant properties help support the body’s natural reaction to these stressors, promoting a healthier-looking complexion.

- Hydrates the SkinBakuchiol is known for its ability to hydrate the skin, enhancing its texture without causing irritation. It helps skin look more even-toned and refreshed, leaving behind a radiant glow.

- Minimizes the Appearance of Fine Lines and WrinklesWith regular use, Bakuchiol can reduce the visual appearance of fine lines and wrinkles, promoting a more youthful and refined complexion.

- Gentle on All Skin TypesUnlike other active ingredients, Bakuchiol is gentle enough for all skin types, including sensitive skin. It delivers results without the irritation, redness, or dryness that some may experience with harsher skincare components.
