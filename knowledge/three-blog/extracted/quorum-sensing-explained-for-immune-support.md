What Is Quorum Sensing?

You might not be familiar with the term “quorum sensing,” but it’s a fascinating process that plays a significant role in how bacteria communicate. In a nutshell, quorum sensing is the way bacteria "talk" to each other. Bad bacteria use this communication process to coordinate their efforts, making it easier for them to spread.

Picture a group of bad bacteria working together like a group of schoolyard bullies. Alone, they might not be able to do much harm, but when they "talk" to each other through quorum sensing, they can form a group strong enough to overwhelm the body.
