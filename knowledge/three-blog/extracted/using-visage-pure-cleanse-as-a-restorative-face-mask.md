Why Use Visage Pure Cleanse as a Face Mask?

Visage Pure Cleanse is packed with skin-loving ingredients that not only cleanse but also help restore and refresh. When used as a face mask, it provides an extra layer of benefits:

- Deep Cleansing: Draws out impurities, leaving skin feeling fresh and smooth.

- Hydration Boost: Helps the skin maintain moisture for a more vibrant look and feel.

- Supports Skin & Microbiome Health: Key ingredients nourish the skin while maintaining its natural balance.

- Gentle Yet Effective: Unlike harsh masks, this formula is soothing, making it perfect even for sensitive skin.

- Antioxidant Support: Supports the skin’s natural reaction to oxidative stress caused by environmental factors like UV rays and pollution.

The unique blend of desert plantain, adzuki bean, and quorum sensing ingredients delivers deep nourishment, making it the perfect choice for a restorative treatment.

How to Use Visage Pure Cleanse as a Restorative Face Mask

Transforming Visage Pure Cleanse into a face mask is a simple apply twice method! Here’s how:

- Start with Clean Skin – Wash your face using Visage Pure Cleanse with lukewarm water and pat dry. This helps prep your skin for maximum absorption.

- Apply a Generous Layer – Smooth an even layer of Visage Pure Cleanse onto your face, avoiding the eye and mouth area.

- Let It Work Its Magic – After application, let the cleanser sit for 15 minutes to allow the active ingredients to deeply nourish and support your skin’s microbiome.

- Rinse and Glow – Use warm water to gently rinse off the mask, then pat your skin dry.

- Follow Up with Skincare – Lock in the benefits by completing the rest of the steps in the Visage Collection, including Visage Radiant Toner and Visage Super Serum.

Pro Tips for the Ultimate Glow-Up

- Apply the mask after a warm shower when your pores are open for better absorption.

- Use a jade roller or gua sha over the mask for a spa-like experience.

- Follow with Visage Super Serum for an extra boost of hydration.

- Try it twice a week for best results!
