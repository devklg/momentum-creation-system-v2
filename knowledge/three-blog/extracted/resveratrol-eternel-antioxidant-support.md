What Is Resveratrol?

Resveratrol is a polyphenolic compound found in the skins of red grapes, blueberries, and other antioxidant-rich plants. In nature, it helps protect plants from environmental stress. In humans, it has been studied for its potential to support a wide range of natural processes, including:

- Heart health

- The body’s response to oxidative stress

- Cellular energy production

- Natural aging and longevity pathways

Because of its antioxidant activity, Resveratrol is often associated with promoting internal balance and vitality—especially in environments impacted by pollution, UV exposure, and lifestyle stressors.
