When it comes to skincare, true beauty starts from within. That’s why combining a high-quality topical serum with internal skin support can make all the difference in achieving radiant, healthy-looking skin. At THREE, we believe in creating products that complement each other, working together to deliver real, impactful results.

Our Visage Super Serum and Collagène are a perfect example of this synergy. These two products form a powerful inside-out approach to skincare, working in harmony to promote collagen production, support skin hydration, and vibrant appearance.

Here are five reasons why Visage Super Serum and Collagène make a powerful duo that can take your skincare routine to the next

1. Double the Collagen Support: Inside and Outside

Collagène is designed to support your body’s natural collagen production from within by providing bioavailable marine-sourced collagen peptides. This helps support the skin from within, promoting elasticity, firmness, and resilience.

Meanwhile, Visage Super Serum contains powerful collagen-supporting ingredients like Bakuchiol, Vitamin C, and Vitex Agnus-Castus, which promote skin hydration, elasticity, and collagen production at the surface level. Bakuchiol is especially renowned for being a gentler alternative to retinol, promoting collagen synthesis without irritation, while Vitamin C enhances the skin’s appearance and supports collagen production by delivering antioxidant support against environmental stressors.

2. Hydration from Both Sides

Collagène supports hydration from within by providing amino acids and peptides that help the skin retain moisture and maintain its natural barrier. Hydration from within is essential for a radiant, glowing complexion.

On the outside, Visage Super Serum delivers deep hydration through ingredients like Squalane and Allantoin. Squalane enhances moisture retention, making the skin feel supple and soft, while Allantoin helps the skin retain moisture, visually smoothing and minimizing the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles.

By combining these two products, you can ensure that your skin is not only hydrated externally but also deeply nourished from within—leading to a hydrated, glowing complexion that lasts.













 -












3. Supporting Skin Elasticity: The Gut-Skin Connection

Collagène does more than just provide healthy support to the skin—it also plays an important role in supporting the gut microbiome through its amino acids. Gut health is closely linked to a healthy complexion, as research shows that the gut microbiome impacts skin health.

Visage Super Serum complements this by supporting the skin’s microbiome, which is vital for maintaining skin balance and supporting against environmental stressors. By nourishing the skin’s microbiome with bioactive ingredients like Coriandrum Sativum, Visage helps promote a healthy, balanced complexion, enhancing skin wellness and vitality.

This dual support for the gut-skin axis ensures that your skin receives comprehensive nourishment, visually enhancing overall texture and appearance.

4. Promotes Healthy Response to Environmental Factors

Both products contain ingredients that help provide healthy support to the skin’s natural reaction to oxidative stress and environmental factors.

Collagène provides antioxidant support from within, helping the body counteract the negative effects of free radicals, which can accelerate the appearance of skin aging.

Visage Super Serum offers external protection with its Miracle Elixir Blend, which includes powerful antioxidants like Vitamin C and Bakuchiol, helping to support the body’s natural reaction to UV exposure and other environmental stressors.

By addressing oxidative stress both internally and externally, the combination of Collagène and Visage supports a more supported, radiant complexion that’s better able to withstand everyday environmental challenges.

5. Youthful, Radiant Skin from Every Angle

When used together, Visage Super Serum and Collagène provide a holistic approach to achieving youthful, radiant skin. While Collagène works from within to support skin health, hydration, and elasticity, Visage Super Serum delivers powerful bioactive ingredients that promote visually smoother, more radiant skin externally.

This inside-outside approach ensures your skin gets the comprehensive support it needs to maintain a healthy, vibrant appearance. With both products addressing different aspects of skin health, you’re giving your skin what it needs to look and feel its best from every angle.

Inside-Outside Synergy for Radiant Skin

By combining Visage Super Serum with Collagène, you’re not only addressing your skin’s external needs but also providing the internal support it requires to look and feel its best. These two products work together to support collagen production, hydrate your skin, and support your skin’s natural reaction to environmental stressors—ensuring that your skincare routine is as comprehensive and effective as possible.

Whether you’re looking to improve your skin’s radiance, support elasticity, or simply support skin health, Visage Super Serum and Collagène offer a powerful, synergistic solution that delivers beauty from within.
