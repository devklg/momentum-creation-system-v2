Tips for Supporting Detoxification at Home

In addition to using Purifí, here are a few practical steps you can take to support your body’s natural detox processes:

- Nourish Your Body with Whole Foods

A balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins can provide the nutrients your body needs to thrive.

Sleep is when your body does its most important repair work. Getting enough rest ensures your detox systems function effectively.
