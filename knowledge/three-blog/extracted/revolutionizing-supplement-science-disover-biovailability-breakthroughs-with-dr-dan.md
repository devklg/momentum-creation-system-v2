The Importance of Bioactives and Cellular Absorption

Bioactives are compounds found in foods and supplements that have an effect on living tissues. Curcumin, the active compound in turmeric, and resveratrol, found in the skins of grapes, are two well-known bioactives. Both have been extensively studied for their potential health benefits, which include supporting a healthy inflammatory response, antioxidant properties, and overall wellness.

However, the effectiveness of these bioactives largely depends on their bioavailability – that is, the extent and rate at which they are absorbed into the bloodstream and utilized by the body. Simply consuming these compounds is not enough; they need to be delivered efficiently to the cells where they can exert their beneficial effects.

Natural Ingredients: Curcumin and Resveratrol

Curcumin and resveratrol are prime examples of bioactives that have significant health potential but face challenges in terms of bioavailability.

Curcumin: Known for its support of a healthy inflammatory response and antioxidant properties, curcumin has a low natural bioavailability. This means that when consumed in its natural form, only a small fraction reaches the bloodstream. Advanced delivery systems, such as those used by THREE International, enhance its absorption, allowing the body to benefit fully from its properties.

Resveratrol: This powerful antioxidant supports cardiovascular health and longevity. Like curcumin, resveratrol also has low bioavailability. By employing cellular absorption technologies, THREE ensures that resveratrol is more effectively absorbed and utilized by the body.

Cellular Absorption Technologies at THREE

At THREE, we utilize a wide range of cutting-edge cellular absorption technologies, 11 in total, to maximize the bioavailability of our products. These technologies help deliver molecules directly to the cells, enhancing their effectiveness. Some of the key technologies we use include:

- Liposomal Delivery Systems: This technology encapsulates bioactives in liposomes (tiny fat-like particles), which enhance their absorption and protect them from degradation as they pass through the digestive system.

- Micronization: By reducing the particle size of bioactives to the micron scale, this technology increases the surface area for absorption, improving the solubility and bioavailability of these compounds.

- Enzyme Delivery Technology: This approach utilizes specific enzymes to facilitate the absorption of bioactives, ensuring that they are effectively broken down and utilized by the body.

- Yeast Glycoprotein Matrix (GPM) Natural Vitamins and Minerals: This innovative technology incorporates vitamins and minerals into a natural yeast matrix, enhancing their bioavailability and making them more readily absorbed by the body.

These advanced delivery systems help ensure that THREE supplements are on the cutting edge of nutrient delivery, providing the maximum wellness benefits from the best ingredients.
