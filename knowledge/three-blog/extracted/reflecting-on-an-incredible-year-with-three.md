As we close the final chapter of this year, I wanted to take a moment to personally thank each and every one of you—our incredible Ambassadors and loyal customers. Your dedication, belief, and support have made this past year at THREE International incredible.

This year, we reached new heights, achieved incredible milestones, and took bold steps forward in our shared journey of proactive wellness. It’s not just the numbers or launches that define our success—it’s the community we’ve built together, rooted in collaboration and a mutual commitment to empowering others to live healthier, more fulfilling lives.

The Launch of the Visage Collection: A Defining Moment

One of the most thrilling moments of this year was the launch of the Visage Collection—a skincare line unlike any other. From the Visage Pure Cleanse, to the Visage Radiant Toner, to our innovative Visage Super Serum, we’ve redefined the concept of skincare by combining nature and advanced science into a comprehensive collection that truly works with your skin.

The Visage Collection embodies everything THREE stands for: cutting-edge innovation, unmatched quality, and the pursuit of excellence in bioavailable wellness. The feedback we’ve received from you—our customers and Ambassadors—has been inspiring. Your testimonials about the transformative results and how these products have become staples in your daily routines mean the world to us. Thank you for welcoming Visage into your lives and for sharing its benefits with others.













 -












Looking Ahead to 2025

While 2024 has been remarkable, I can’t help but feel an incredible sense of anticipation for what lies ahead in 2025. We’ve been hard at work behind the scenes, and I can confidently say that the best is yet to come. From new product launches to exciting community events, we are gearing up for a year filled with opportunities to grow, innovate, and thrive together.

More than anything, we’re committed to ensuring that THREE continues to deliver products, education, and support that enhance your well-being. Our mission to bring proactive wellness to the world will only grow stronger in the coming year, and we can’t wait to have you by our side every step of the way.

Gratitude for Our Community

To our Ambassadors: You are the heart of this movement. Your passion and dedication to sharing THREE with the world are what drive us forward. Your belief in our mission fuels everything we do. Thank you for your leadership, your vision, and your commitment to improving lives—not just your own, but the lives of so many others.

To our customers: You are the reason we do what we do. Your trust in our products and your stories of how they’ve made a difference inspire us daily. Thank you for choosing THREE and for being a vital part of this growing global community.













 -












A Shared Vision

As we prepare to turn the page to 2025, I want to leave you with this: THREE International is not just a company—it’s a movement. This community is changing lives on a daily basis. It is amazing to see what can happen when a committed group of leaders comes together with a singular focus of helping others.

From myself and the entire THREE International team, we wish you a happy and healthy holiday season and an incredible start to the New Year. Let’s continue to rise up, innovate, and make 2025 an unforgettable year.

With gratitude and love,

Daniel Picou

CEO, THREE International
