What is Liquid Collagen and Why is it Different?

Collagen is the most abundant protein in our body, essential for supporting the health of the skin, joints, and body. Unfortunately, our natural collagen production slows down as we age, leading to visible signs of aging like dull skin and fine lines.

That’s where supplementation comes in. Collagen supplements typically come in three main forms: powder, capsules, and liquid. While all can be beneficial, liquid collagen stands out for its faster absorption and easier digestion—meaning your body can put it to work more quickly.

There are also different sources of collagen:

- Bovine collagen: derived from cows and rich in Types I and III collagen.

- Marine collagen: derived from fish, with a smaller molecular size for greater bioavailability.
