The world of skincare is always evolving, but some ingredients stand the test of time while others emerge as game-changers. Whether you’re new to skincare or a devoted enthusiast, understanding which ingredients to look for can help you make smarter choices for your skin. At THREE, we’ve carefully selected powerful, science-backed ingredients to create The Visage Collection, ensuring your skincare routine delivers real results. Here are the top five must-have skincare ingredients for 2025 that everyone should have on their radar.

1. Bakuchiol: The Natural Skin Refiner

Bakuchiol has taken the skincare world by storm—and for good reason. This plant-based ingredient supports collagen production, hydrates, and minimizes the appearance of fine lines over time. Found in the Visage Super Serum, it delivers all these benefits without irritation, making it suitable for all skin types.

2. Niacinamide: The Radiance Booster

Niacinamide, also known as Vitamin B3, is a powerhouse for enhancing your skin’s natural radiance. It helps promote even skin tone, reduce the appearance of pores, and visually improve the skin’s overall texture. In the Visage Radiant Toner, niacinamide plays a key role in prepping your skin for maximum absorption of the products that follow.

3. Allantoin: The Hydration Hero

Allantoin is celebrated for its ability to support the skin’s moisture retention, leaving it feeling soft and smooth. It also promotes a soothing effect, making it perfect for sensitive skin. Visage Pure Cleanse utilizes allantoin to gently cleanse while maintaining the skin’s essential moisture.













 -












4. Beta-Glucan: The Skin Barrier Supporter

Beta-Glucan is a natural compound known for its ability to soothe, hydrate, and support the skin’s natural barrier. It works to help skin stay hydrated and comfortable, even in harsh environmental conditions. Found in the Visage Radiant Toner, Beta-Glucan enhances hydration while supporting your skin’s overall resilience.

5. Magnesium Ascorbyl Phosphate (Vitamin C): The Brightening Agent

A gentle, stabilized form of Vitamin C, Magnesium Ascorbyl Phosphate helps support your skin's antioxidant response while visually improving the appearance of dull skin. Found in the Visage Radiant Toner, this form of Vitamin C promotes a more radiant and even complexion while offering antioxidant aid in the skin’s natural reaction to environmental stressors.













 -












6. Mountain Caviar: The Luxe Skin Revitalizer

Derived from the nutrient-rich seeds of alpine plants, Mountain Caviar is the standout ingredient in Visage Crème Caviar. It delivers concentrated botanical nutrients that help improve skin firmness, enhance hydration, and promote a more youthful appearance. Known for its silky texture and rejuvenating properties, Mountain Caviar brings a luxurious boost to your skincare ritual.
