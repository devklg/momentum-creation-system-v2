The Power of Bioavailability: Why It Matters

Bioavailability is a term you may have heard, but why is it so important?

In simple terms, bioavailability refers to how well and how quickly nutrients are absorbed and utilized by the body. When it comes to supplements, bioavailability is critical—after all, it’s not just about what’s in a product, but how much of it your body can actually use.

THREE takes bioavailability to the next level by integrating Cellular Absorption Technologies into our products.

These innovative techniques ensure that the active ingredients in our supplements reach your cells quickly and efficiently, making our products more effective than traditional supplements.

Cellular Absorption Technologies: The Heart of Our Formulations

At THREE, we use 11 Cellular Absorption Technologies across our product line, each designed to optimize how nutrients are delivered and absorbed by the body.

These technologies work together to enhance the efficacy of each product and ensure that your body can access the full range of benefits that each formula offers.

Some of the key technologies include:

Liposomes: Used in products like Purifí, Revíve, and Imúne, liposomes are microscopic spheres that encapsulate nutrients, allowing them to be absorbed more effectively at the cellular level.

Micelle and Adjuvants: These technologies, found in Revíve, help improve the solubility and absorption of fat-soluble nutrients, making them more bioavailable.

Yeast Glycoprotein Matrix (GPM): Found in Vitalité, this natural delivery system enhances the absorption of vitamins and minerals through a glycoprotein matrix that mimics the body's own processes.

Polyphenol Complexation and Hydrolysis: Used in Collagène and Éternel, these methods enhance the absorption of antioxidants, collagen, and other bioactive compounds to support skin and cellular health.

Chelated Amino Acids and Fulvic Acid: Found in Imúne and Purifí, these ingredients play a key role in nutrient transport and detoxification.

Together, these technologies are at the core of what makes THREE’s products stand out in the wellness industry. We’ve redefined what it means to create supplements that don’t just “work”—they’re designed for optimal absorption at the cellular level.
