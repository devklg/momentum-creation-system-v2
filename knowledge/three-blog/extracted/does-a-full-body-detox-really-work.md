Our bodies are equipped with an incredible built-in cleansing system. How Detoxification Works Inside the Body

Whether it’s through the food we eat, the air we breathe, or the things we touch, our bodies are exposed to toxins on a daily basis. Luckily, we are equipped with sophisticated organs and systems that are designed to eliminate toxins from the body.

Each elimination organ contributes to detoxification in its own unique way:

- Digestive tract - processes waste products with the help of other organs and expels them from the body.

- Liver - converts toxins into less harmful forms and then eliminates them through the urinary and digestive tracts.

- Kidneys - filter blood to remove waste.

- Lungs - our lungs trap and expel inhaled particles through a built-in filtration system.

- Skin - acts as a barrier and eliminates toxins through sweat.

While our bodies are able to naturally eliminate toxins, our elimination organs must be functioning effectively for these processes to occur. While quick fixes are not the solution, there are ways to support and optimize our body’s natural detoxification, including:

- Getting enough quality sleep

- Drinking adequate amounts of water

- Eating a balanced diet

- Exercising regularly

- Supporting elimination organs through quality supplements
