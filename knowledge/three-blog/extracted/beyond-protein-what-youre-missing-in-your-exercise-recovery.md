Recovering from exercise and strength training is an essential part of building healthy muscles and keeping your body fit. Proper rest and recovery enable your body to build and maintain muscle. But exercise recovery is not just all about protein.

If you only focus on protein, you may be missing the bigger picture of true exercise recovery.
