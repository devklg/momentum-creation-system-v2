At THREE, we’re all about taking proactive steps—whether it’s for your health, your community, or the planet. With National Recycling Day fresh in our minds, it’s the perfect time to think about how we can contribute to a more sustainable world. And here’s the best part: making an eco-friendly impact doesn’t have to be complicated. Something as simple as reusing or recycling your THREE supplement jars can make a meaningful difference.

Instead of tossing your empty jars, why not think outside the jar and give them a second life? These sturdy containers are not only designed to hold our wellness supplements—they’re also perfect for a variety of creative and practical uses. Whether you’re a fan of DIY projects or just want to cut down on waste, here are some fun ways to repurpose your jars and make your home a little greener.

Creative Ways to Reuse Your Supplement Jars

- Craft Room Superstar

Keep your craft supplies organized with ease! Use your jars to store buttons, beads, glitter, or even rolls of string. Label the jars for extra flair and a touch of personalization. Not only will your craft station look tidy, but you’ll also have easy access to all your materials.

- Snack-Sized Storage

Who needs plastic baggies when you have the perfect snack-sized container? Fill your jars with nuts, dried fruit, or even small candies to keep in your bag or desk drawer. These jars are durable, compact, and an excellent alternative for reducing single-use plastic waste.

- The Office Essential

Say goodbye to desk clutter by using your jars as storage for paperclips, thumbtacks, or rubber bands. Their sleek, uniform design keeps your workspace looking clean and organized while helping you reduce waste.

- Bathroom Buddy

Empty jars are a lifesaver when it comes to organizing your bathroom. Use them to store cotton balls, cotton swabs, or even hair ties. Bonus points for labeling them for a spa-like aesthetic.

- Travel-Friendly Containers

Heading on a trip? Use the jars to store small essentials like jewelry, vitamins, or skincare samples. They’re lightweight, secure, and fit perfectly in your travel bag. Plus, they protect your items from spills and damage.

- DIY Plant Pots

Add a touch of greenery to your home by turning your jars into mini planters. Perfect for small succulents or herbs, these jars can brighten up your kitchen or windowsill while promoting a love for sustainable living.

- Household Hero

Jars are a great solution for organizing tiny odds and ends, such as batteries, screws, or keys. Keep them in your kitchen drawer or garage so you’ll never lose track of those small yet essential items again.













 -












Recycling: The Last Resort

If you’re not planning to reuse your jars, recycling is the next best step! THREE’s supplement jars are made from recyclable materials, so be sure to clean them out and place them in your recycling bin. Proper recycling ensures that the materials can be reused to create something new instead of sitting in a landfill for years to come.

Pro tip: Check your local recycling guidelines to make sure plastic jars are accepted in your area. Some regions may require you to remove the label first, so take a few extra seconds to do so if needed!

Why It Matters

It’s easy to underestimate the impact of small actions, but the truth is, every little effort adds up. By reusing and recycling your supplement jars, you’re not only reducing waste but also contributing to a culture of sustainability. It’s about being mindful of the choices we make and finding joy in the simple, proactive steps that help protect our planet.

At THREE, we believe wellness extends beyond the body. It’s about supporting the health of our communities and the environment we share. When we take care of the earth, we’re taking care of ourselves—and that’s a mission we can all get behind.

Join Us in Making a Difference

Ready to give your jars a new purpose? Start small. Pick one creative way to repurpose your jars or make recycling a habit. Encourage your friends and family to do the same. Together, we can create a ripple effect that inspires others to think differently about waste.

Let’s continue to lead by example, demonstrating that proactive wellness doesn’t stop at our bodies—it’s about supporting the world we live in too. So, grab those empty jars, get creative, and think outside the jar!
