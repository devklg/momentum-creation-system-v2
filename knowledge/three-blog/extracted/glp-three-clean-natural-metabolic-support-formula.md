Saffron Extract (Safranal & Crocin)

Saffron has been revered across cultures and centuries for its remarkable properties, and for good reason. The active compounds in saffron, safranal, and crocin, bring both functional and sensory value to the GLP THREETM formula.

Safranal and crocin are the same compounds responsible for saffron’s signature golden hue, which means the warm yellow color you see in every bottle of GLP THREE isn’t from added dyes, it’s nature doing what it does beautifully. In the formula, saffron extract works in harmony with the other key ingredients to support your overall sense of well-being as part of a healthy routine.

Panax Ginseng Extract (Ginsenosides)

Panax ginseng is one of the most well-regarded botanicals in traditional wellness practices, valued for centuries across Eastern cultures. Its key active molecules, ginsenosides, are what give this root its distinctive functional character.

In GLP THREETM, Panax ginseng extract contributes to the formula’s support of healthy metabolic function and body composition. It’s an ingredient with deep roots, and one that fits naturally within a formula built for modern wellness.

Hops Extract (Humulus Lupulus)

Most people associate hops with beer, but the Humulus lupulus plant has a wellness story all on its own. The bitter acids found in hops extract are the active compounds at work in GLP THREE.

These bitter acids play a meaningful role in rounding out the formula’s approach to supporting metabolic function and satiety. It’s a plant-based ingredient that earns its place in this lineup, and a reminder that nature’s pantry holds more than we sometimes give it credit for.
