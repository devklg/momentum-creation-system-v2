Why Toner Matters in Your Skincare Routine

Let’s start with the basics. Why should you even bother with toner? Here are some of the benefits of adding this step to your daily regimen:

- Hydration: Toners help replenish the skin after cleansing, adding a layer of hydration before applying serums or moisturizers.

- pH Balance: Cleansing can disrupt your skin’s natural pH levels. A toner helps restore balance, which is key for maintaining a healthier-looking complexion.

- Preps Your Skin: Toner primes your skin to better absorb the products that follow, ensuring your serums and moisturizers are as effective as possible.

- Gently Removes Residual Impurities: Even after cleansing, there may be lingering impurities or excess oils on your skin. Toner gently sweeps these away.
