Let’s face it: life is busy. But when it comes to skincare, a few intentional minutes before bed can completely change the way your skin looks and feels. The secret? A focused, high-performance nighttime ritual powered by the Visage Skincare Collection.

In just three minutes, you can deliver luxurious, clinically backed ingredients to your skin that work while you sleep—so you wake up to a visually smoother, more radiant complexion.

Here’s how to create your own nighttime transformation, step by step.

Step 1: Start With a Clean Slate (60 seconds)

Cleansing isn’t just about removing makeup—it’s about giving your skin a clean canvas so every step that follows can do its job.

Start with Visage Pure Cleanse, a gentle, non-stripping formula designed to lift away dirt, oil, and impurities without disrupting your skin’s natural barrier. It’s the ideal first step to prepare your skin for the active ingredients that follow.

At night, your skin enters its renewal phase. A fresh start sets the tone.

Pro Tip: Use lukewarm water to prevent overdrying and maintain your skin’s natural hydration balance.

Step 2: Tone & Prep With Visage Radiant Toner (30 seconds)

This isn’t your average toner. The Visage Radiant Toner delivers hydration and balance with a luxurious botanical blend that prepares the skin for maximum absorption in the steps that follow.

It helps:

- Refresh and rebalance post-cleanse

- Support hydration with every spray

- Prime skin for optimal serum and cream layering

Mist evenly over clean face, holding bottle 6-8 inches away with eyes closed.

Step 3: Elevate With Visage Super Serum (30 seconds)

Now that your skin is prepped, it’s time for targeted performance.

The Visage Super Serum is infused with neurocosmetic ingredients and advanced actives that support skin vitality and visible smoothness. It helps support tone and texture while delivering a rejuvenating boost beneath the surface.

Use a few drops and massage into your face and neck using a gentle upward motion, allowing the lightweight formula to fully absorb.

Step 4: Seal in Luxury With Visage Crème Caviar (30 seconds)

End your ritual with the rich, velvety Visage Crème Caviar, featuring Mountain Caviar—a rare botanical known for its antioxidant support and deeply moisturizing properties.

This cream:

- Replenishes hydration overnight

- Softens the appearance of fine lines

- Wraps skin in a nourishing, luxe finish

Apply a pearl-sized amount to skin and gently massage onto your face, neck and across chest using upward motions.

Step 5: Pause & Let It All Sink In (30 seconds)

Take a breath. You’ve done something wonderful for your skin—and yourself. Let the ingredients do their work as you wind down for the evening.

This isn’t just skincare. It’s self-care in its most refined form.
