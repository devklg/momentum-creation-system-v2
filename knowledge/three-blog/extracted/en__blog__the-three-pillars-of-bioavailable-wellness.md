In a world teeming with health and wellness products, it can be challenging to navigate the sea of choices and find solutions that genuinely make a difference in our well-being. Enter THREE, the global leader in bioavailable wellness products, where science meets proactive wellness, guided by three fundamental pillars: Exploration, Curation, and Absorption. These pillars are the cornerstone of everything we do, ensuring that our offerings provide the most effective proactive wellness solutions available. Let's dive deeper into these pillars and discover how they shape our approach to wellness.

Exploration: Seeking the Best Ingredients

The journey to exceptional wellness products begins with exploration. We believe that the most potent ingredients can be found across the globe, and our commitment to ingredient discovery sets us apart. We scour the world, from lush rainforests to remote mountain ranges, in search of the most innovative, effective, and sustainable components that nature offers.

Our rigorous exploration process is driven by the desire to source ingredients that have a profound impact on well-being. This journey of discovery extends to all corners of the globe, ensuring that our formulations incorporate elements not readily available elsewhere. Whether it's a lush rainforest in Thailand or the Himalayan foothills, we leave no stone unturned in our quest to find nature's most potent treasures.

Curation: Crafting Tailored Solutions

Exploration is only the beginning of our wellness journey. Once we've gathered these exceptional ingredients, we move to the pillar of curation. We understand that every individual's body and needs are unique, and a one-size-fits-all approach to wellness doesn't work. That's why each of our products is carefully curated to provide targeted benefits that cater to specific requirements.

Our commitment to curation means that our products aren't generic solutions. Instead, they are meticulously formulated by our Scientific Advisory Board to address the distinct needs of those who trust THREE for their wellness journey. Tailored solutions are at the core of what we do, and they are our promise to you – a promise that you'll find products that genuinely work for you, guiding you toward the path of holistic health and proactive wellness.

Absorption: Maximizing Nutrient Delivery

The last but equally crucial pillar is absorption. After exploring the world for the best ingredients and curating products for tailored wellness solutions, we go a step further by ensuring that these nutrients reach their intended destination: your cells.

Our innovative Cellular Absorption Technologies play a pivotal role in enhancing nutrient absorption. We believe that the real benefits of any wellness product come from its ability to deliver nutrients at the cellular level. That's why our formulations are engineered to optimize bioavailability and backed by clinical studies, allowing your body to make the most of the nutrients you're giving it.

Through this pillar of absorption, we ensure that you receive the full potential of the ingredients you've come to trust from THREE. Our dedication to delivering nutrients where they matter most sets us apart, ensuring that you're truly benefiting from what you put into your body.

Prescriber’s Digital Reference (PDR): Elevating Trust and Accessibility

At THREE, our commitment to providing proactive wellness solutions is unwavering, and it extends to ensuring that healthcare professionals and patients alike have easy access to the wealth of information about our products. As of April 5, 2023 the entire THREE product line can be found in the prestigious Prescriber’s Digital Reference (PDR).

For those who might not be familiar with the PDR, it holds an esteemed position in the realm of pharmaceutical and nutraceutical product information, alongside being a go-to source for prescription drug data. The PDR is a trusted and commonly used reference by healthcare professionals and patients across the United States. It's more than just a reference; it's a gateway to understanding the products that could shape the path to your proactive wellness.

Listing our supplements in the PDR is not just about recognition; it's about ensuring that healthcare professionals have access to well-researched, scientifically validated, and highly effective wellness options for their patients. This inclusion in the PDR underlines our commitment to providing proactive wellness solutions of the highest quality, rooted in science and designed to empower individuals on their health journey.

By being part of the PDR, we're reaching a broader audience and making our products more accessible to those who seek proactive wellness options. Through this valuable resource, healthcare professionals from various fields can now delve into the rich world of THREE's offerings, exploring the benefits of our formulations for the well-being of their patients.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the three pillars of Exploration, Curation, and Absorption are at the heart of everything we do at THREE. We're not just a wellness brand; we're a proactive wellness movement guided by a commitment to discovery, personalization, and optimum nutrient delivery. In this pursuit, we empower you to redefine what's possible – for your health, well-being, and your life.

At THREE, it's not just about products; it's about a wellness philosophy. It's about improving the lives of those who have chosen to take control of their well-being and embark on a journey of proactive wellness. With the three pillars as our foundation, we're here to support you every step of the way. Join us on this voyage to supported health, greater wellness, and more profound hope.
