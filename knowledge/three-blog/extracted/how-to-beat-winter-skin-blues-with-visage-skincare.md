Why Does Winter Affect Your Skin So Much?

Winter weather often brings drier air and lower humidity, which can strip your skin of its natural moisture. Couple that with indoor heating (which pulls moisture out of the air) and the tendency to take long, hot showers to warm up, and it’s no wonder your skin feels parched and lackluster. Without proper care, winter conditions can take a toll on the skin's appearance.

Proactive Skincare Tips for Winter

In addition to the Visage Collection, here are some quick tips to keep your skin feeling its best during the winter months:

- Stay Hydrated: Drink plenty of water to support your skin’s hydration from within.

- Use a Humidifier: Combat dry indoor air by adding moisture back into your home environment.

- Opt for Lukewarm Showers: As tempting as hot showers are, they can dry out your skin. Stick to warm water instead.

- Apply Skincare to Damp Skin: This helps your products absorb more effectively.
