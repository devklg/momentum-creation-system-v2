 Build Two Teams

 You must build two retail businesses — a Left Leg and a Right Leg. This is the heartbeat of binary compensation. One person alone can only do so much. Two teams multiply everything.




 Teams Build Volume

 As your team grows and orders product, CV accumulates on both legs. You don't need to personally generate thousands of CV — your organization does it collectively, every order, every month. Every product in the catalog generates CV.




 Volume Creates Cycles

 When 300 CV accumulates on the lesser leg and 600 CV on the greater leg (900 CV total), a cycle fires. Either leg can be the lesser or greater — it works both directions.




 Cycles Pay Weekly

 Up to 600 cycles per week at $35 each = $21,000 weekly max. To qualify, a BA must be active and in compliance with the Rewards Plan. The only ceiling is how much volume your two-team organization generates. This is why building deep matters.

 No Breakage

 Volume Flows Up From Unlimited Depth

 One of the most powerful — and most misunderstood — features of the THREE binary is this: there is no breakage. In many compensation structures, volume generated beyond a certain depth is "lost" — it never reaches you. THREE does not work that way.


 What "No Breakage" Means

 Every product purchased by every person in your organization — at any depth, on any level — generates CV that flows upward through the binary tree. Level 1, Level 7, Level 14 — it all counts.This means you can build a team of substantial, even extraordinary size and every person in that organization supports your income through the volume they generate. The deeper the team grows, the more volume accumulates on your legs, the more cycles fire, and the more you earn.Unlike a traditional job where your income is capped by what you personally produce, the binary allows your entire organization's productivity to count toward your compensation. This is why team building is the foundation — because the team's volume never breaks away from you.








 First Mover Advantage

 Why Position in the Tree Matters

 In a binary compensation plan, timing is a structural advantage — not just motivation. The sooner you enroll and the earlier your position is established in your sponsor's tree, the greater the compounding benefit you receive as the organization grows.


 Early Enrollee
First Mover Position

✓ Placed higher in the binary tree
✓ As your upline builds their organization, enrollees placed in the shared leg fill positions below yours — their CV flows upward through your position
✓ Every person enrolled after you in your sponsor's tree builds volume below your position
✓ The shared leg fills from the bottom up as your sponsor and upline build — every placement below your position flows CV upward through you

 VS

 Later Enrollee
Building Under You

• Placed deeper in the tree, below early enrollees
• Their volume and their team's volume flow upward — benefiting those positioned above
• They still earn — but they are contributing to the volume and cycles of those who came before them
• Their early enrollees benefit the same way — the advantage compounds with every generation



 The bottom line: The sooner you enroll and begin building, the more of the organization's future growth flows through your position. This is not a sales tactic — it is structural math. Every person who enrolls after you, at any depth below you, contributes volume that supports your cycles. This is the first mover advantage built into every binary plan.
