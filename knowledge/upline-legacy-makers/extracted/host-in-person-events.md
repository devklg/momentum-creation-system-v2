## How to Host Incredible In-Person Events &amp; Look Like a Pro

## Why Host In-Person Events?

- Build stronger connections in your community and team
- Share the THREE story with impact
- Create trust &amp; excitement around the products and business

## Enroll New Brand Ambassadors and Build a Winning team!

## Before the Event

- Pick a comfortable location (home, coffee shop, community room) ✔
- Send personal invites 3-7 days before: Custom Invites can be edited on www.rlegacymakers.com ✔
- Prepare a simple agenda (30-60 mins) ✔
- Have samples ready (Eternal, Kynetik, Collagen, etc.) ✔

## Hosting Like a Pro Agenda

- Welcome Everyone Warmly : Smile, make them feel at home, make sure your home is clean and animals are put away. The less Distractions the better.
- Share Your Story : Why YOU said YES to THREE.
- Show the Products : Display and Let them taste/try and share benefits.
- Use Tools : Play a Pre-Recorded Presentation Video or perform a Live Presentation using the team slides and have brochures and Product usage Guide for credibility printed out to share.
- Q&amp;A : &amp; Next Steps: Invite them to try products and join your team, Pass out printed enrollment forms.

## Etiquette Tips (Pro Moves)

- Start and end on time
- Dress the part (confident &amp; approachable)
- Be authentic - people buy YOU before the product
- Always follow up within 24 hours
- -Announce and Invite them to the next event either online or in-person.

## Quick Pro Checklist

- Invites sent ■
- Samples ready ■
- Agenda, Product Usage Guide, Family of Products and Enrollment Forms Printed ■
- Testimonials lined up- Always a good Idea to have 1-2 existing teammates share their story ■
- Follow-up scheduled ■

Confidence comes with clarity. Use this guide to keep it simple, fun, and impactful. You've got this!
