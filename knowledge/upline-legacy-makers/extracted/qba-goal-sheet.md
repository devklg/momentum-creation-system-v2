1 cycle is rewarded when you have an accumulation of 300 CV on either Left or Right teams and 600 CV on the opposite team, either Left/Right teams, totaling 900 CV between both Left and Right Teams = 1 Cycle.  1 Cycle = 900 CV Total.

(Calculate what is needed by using your 'Rank Information' Dial in your Back Office.)

1 Cycle = a $35 commission per cycle.

Happy 10 1 Cycle = a $45 commission per cycle. (Personally Sponsor a combo of 10 BA's and Customers in a 4 week period)

In Three's Binary compensation plan, you can earn up to 500 cycles ($17,500) per week without a Happy 10. With a Happy 10 (when you have a total of 10 or more customers and or brand ambassadors in a 4 week rolling period) you can earn up to 600 cycles ($27,000) per week.
