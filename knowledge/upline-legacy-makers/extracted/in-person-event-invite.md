5 polished Private Business Reception (PBR) style invites you can use for in-home presentations, coffee shop meetups, or casual private gatherings . These are designed to feel warm, exclusive, low-pressure, and curiosity-driven.

<!-- image -->

## Invite #1 - Personal &amp; Warm

Hi {Name},

I'm hosting a small private gathering with a few friends this week to share something I've recently gotten involved with that I'm really excited about.

It's super casual - just conversation, connection, and a chance to see what I've been working on lately. You came to mind because I value your perspective.

Would you be open to joining us?

## Invite #2 - Coffee Shop Meetup Style

Hey {Name},

I'm meeting with a few friends at a local coffee shop for a relaxed private business reception to share something new I've been building in wellness and entrepreneurship.

No pressure at all - just good coffee, great people, and ideas worth hearing about. I'd love for you to come sit with us if you're available.
