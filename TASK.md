## THE TEACHING MANDATE FOR THE COMP MODULES (this is the JOB, not just listing facts)

THREE International tracks all volume in its own back office. The app NEVER computes CV. The training's job is purely CONCEPTUAL — teach the BA three things, in this order:

1. **What CV is and where it comes from.** Commissionable Volume is attached to every product; it is generated by every order from every person (BA or customer) anywhere in the organization, on either leg, at any depth. The BA does not personally generate thousands of CV — the organization does it collectively, every order, every month.
2. **How volume becomes a check.** 300 CV (lesser leg) + 600 CV (greater leg) = 900 CV = one cycle = $35, paid weekly, up to the cap. Active (100 CV enroll + 60 CV/28 days) and Qualified (one BA left, one BA right) to earn.
3. **THE BIG ONE — make the exponential REALIZABLE, not fantastical.** This is the heart of the comp training. The "$21K/week" number sounds impossible to a beginner. The job is to make it feel REACHABLE by showing it is built from ONE small repeated act: you sponsor 2 (one left, one right) and TEACH them to do the same. Their 2 teach their 2. Fourteen rounds of that single behavior = 16,384 people generating volume — and you never personally recruited more than 2. "How do you eat an elephant? One bite at a time." The BA is not asked to do something huge; they are asked to do one small thing and duplicate it. The exponential is what the SYSTEM does when each person does the small thing. No breakage (volume flows up from unlimited depth) and first-mover advantage (earlier position = more of the org's future growth flows through you) are the two structural facts that make the duplication real, not hype.

The BA should leave the comp modules thinking: "I don't have to find thousands of people. I have to find 2 and teach them what I just learned. That I can do." That belief is the deliverable.
