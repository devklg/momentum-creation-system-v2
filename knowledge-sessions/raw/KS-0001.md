# Executive Summary

This session captures the architectural breakthrough that fundamentally changed the design of Momentum Creation System V2.

Momentum is not an application centered around AI agents.

Momentum is a knowledge-centric platform.

The purpose of every conversation, interview, CRM interaction, training session, and coaching experience is to create organizational knowledge that helps future Brand Ambassadors succeed.

The Knowledge Core becomes the organizational memory of Team Magnificent.

Every AI agent becomes a specialist that contributes to and consumes that shared knowledge.

---

# Background

The original architecture focused on agents.

As discussion progressed it became clear that the real value of Momentum is not the individual agents.

The real value is the growing organizational understanding created through conversations with Brand Ambassadors.

This insight fundamentally changes the platform architecture.

---

# Architectural Discovery

The platform is not:

Agent-centric.

It is:

Knowledge-centric.

Agents become consumers and contributors to organizational knowledge.

Knowledge becomes the permanent asset.

# Core Principle

Context creates guidance.

Guidance creates action.

Action creates outcomes.

Outcomes create learning.

Learning expands organizational knowledge.

# Major Decisions

## Decision 1

Momentum is a Knowledge-Centric Platform.

The Knowledge Core becomes the center of the application.

Agents become clients of the Knowledge Core.

---

## Decision 2

## Decision 3

Templates guide every interview.

Agents never invent interviews.

Each interview follows a versioned template.

Examples:

Michael Training Interview

Ivory Opportunity Map Interview

Templates evolve under governance.

# Architectural Insight

The platform should not continually become more complicated.

Instead:

Knowledge should continually become richer.

This allows every future agent to improve without changing its fundamental purpose.
