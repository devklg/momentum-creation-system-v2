CLARIFICATION 8 — PERSONAL WORD-OF-MOUTH ADVERTISING AT TEAM SCALE

Kevin: This is your personal advertising — word-of-mouth. Our sharers. This tool will allow us, as a team, to literally send out tens of thousands of video shares daily.

Claude locked this. The Momentum Creation System is a personal word-of-mouth advertising platform at team scale. Brand Ambassadors are SHARERS — the advertising channel. Word-of-mouth is the oldest, most trusted form of advertising; the system turns that channel into something that scales.

Volume math at team scale:
  - 100 active BAs × 5 shares/day = 500/day = 15,000/month
  - 1,000 BAs × 5 = 5,000/day = 150,000/month
  - 10,000 BAs × 5 = 50,000/day = 1,500,000/month

Tens of thousands of video shares daily is not a stretch goal — it's a math problem the system solves once the team reaches scale. Architecture must handle the volume from day one. Triple-stack writes per event, SSE streams, gateway-mediated persistence — these were the right choices.

CLARIFICATION 12 — ELEGANCE IS SIMPLICITY

Kevin: This is an elegant system in its simplicity, because all we're doing is sharing the video, not trying to convince, not trying to persuade, manipulate. All we're doing is sharing information to let a person make up their mind, moving on to the next.

Claude locked this. The elegance of the Momentum Creation System is its simplicity:

  - BA does ONE thing: share the video. Not convince. Not persuade. Not manipulate. Not handle objections. Not close. Not chase. Just share.
  - Prospect does one thing: watch or don't, decide or don't — their call.
  - BA moves on to the next.

Why this is the unlock: the moment a BA's job is "share, then move on," everything that historically broke network marketing — high-pressure pitches, relationship damage, dread of bringing it up, burnout from chasing — goes away. The BA does not carry the weight of someone else's decision.

Why it works: respects the prospect, protects the BA, scales (sharing scales, convincing doesn't), compounds (every share counts equally), produces the volume goal naturally.

The frame in one line: Share information. Respect the decision. Move on. Repeat.

The 8-week flush is the system's version of "move on" — a prospect who hasn't decided in 8 weeks gets flushed, gently, without judgment.

What this rules out: AI prospecting, autodialing, mass send, manipulation tactics, scripted objection handling, going-for-the-close. The system refuses these not because of compliance but because they break the elegance.
