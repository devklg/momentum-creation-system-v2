The three surfaces, one language

  • teammagnificent.com — prospect-facing. Replicated BA site and replicated prospect dashboard at /p/{token}. Cinematic energy.

— H  ·  logo system

The compass rose.

The Team Magnificent logo is a compass rose: gold star points, teal center dot, blue compass lines. It is the brand mark of every surface in the system.

— the brand
— locked The logo is never redesigned. The compass rose is the artifact; the world around it evolves. Decision from Chat #2.

What never to do with the logo

  • Never recolor the compass rose. Gold points and teal center are fixed.
  • Never rotate. The compass orientation is the brand orientation.
  • Never apply effects: no drop shadow, no glow, no outline, no blur.
  • Never place on a light gray background. Designed for ink or white only.
  • Never substitute a custom emoji, icon, or AI-generated mark.
— I  ·  intensity tiers

One language. Three registers.

Same design language, three intensity registers. The differences are dials, not departures.

01
tier one
.com cinematic
Full atmosphere. Headlines at 112px on wide screens. Staggered entrance animation, position card with gold-bright spotlight, live ticker always visible.
02
tier two
.team focused
Same atmosphere on body, content density goes up. Headlines moderate. Entrance animation reserved for milestones: welcome, Michael complete, first invite sent.
03
tier three
admin dense
Full body atmosphere stays. Data-dense regions sit on solid ink-2 panels. Headlines smaller. Tables get hairline gold dividers.
