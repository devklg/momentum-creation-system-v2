# PURPOSE

The New Brand Ambassador Discovery & Success Interview is the first personalized experience within the Brand Ambassador Success System.

Its purpose is not evaluation.

Its purpose is understanding.

Its purpose is not qualification.

Its purpose is preparation.

Its purpose is not prediction.

Its purpose is personalization.

The interview serves as the bridge between enrollment and successful participation within the Team Magnificent ecosystem.

The interview creates the foundation for:

* Personalization
* Orientation
* Launch Planning
* Coaching
* Mentorship
* AI Guidance
* Community Placement
* Long-Term Success

---

# GUIDING PHILOSOPHY

## Core Principle

Every individual arrives with a unique story.

The interview exists to understand that story.

The system assumes:

* Every person has strengths.
* Every person has potential.
* Every person learns differently.
* Every person defines success differently.

The interview shall never attempt to:

* Rank people
* Predict outcomes
* Determine worth
* Judge capability
* Limit opportunity

Instead, the interview exists to uncover:

* Motivation
* Vision
* Experience
* Confidence
* Goals
* Learning Preferences
* Support Needs

---

# INTERVIEW OBJECTIVES

The Discovery Interview has four primary objectives.

## Objective 1: Understand

Understand the person.

Not the prospect.

Not the recruit.

The person.

---

## Objective 2: Personalize

Create an individualized success path.

No two journeys should be identical.

---

## Objective 3: Support

Identify areas where support may accelerate confidence and momentum.

---

## Objective 4: Prepare

Prepare the individual for a successful launch experience.

---

# USER EXPERIENCE DESIGN

The experience should feel like:

* A conversation
* A welcome
* A mentorship session

It should never feel like:

* An application
* An examination
* An interrogation
* A survey

The participant should feel:

* Seen
* Heard
* Understood
* Valued

---

# INTERVIEW FLOW

Total Duration Target:

10–15 Minutes

---

## Phase 1 — Welcome

Purpose:

Reduce anxiety.

Establish trust.

Sample Opening:

"Welcome to Team Magnificent.

Before we begin your journey, we'd love to learn a little more about you so we can support you in the best possible way.

There are no right or wrong answers.

This conversation simply helps us understand your goals, interests, and how we can best serve you."

---

## Phase 2 — Personal Discovery

### Question 1

What inspired you to become a Brand Ambassador?

---

### Question 2

What excites you most about this opportunity?

---

### Question 3

What would success look like for you personally?

---

### Dynamic Follow-Up

Why is that important to you?

Continue:

"Tell me more."

Maximum depth:

3 why levels.
