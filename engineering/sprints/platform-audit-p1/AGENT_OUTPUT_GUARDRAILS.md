# Agent Output Guardrails

P1-61 adds a cross-agent QA suite covering the platform skill/template registry
and the deterministic generated-copy boundary.

The suite proves:

- AI scoring/qualification and income language are rejected by the shared scanner;
- Steve, Michael, and Ivory declare scoring or qualification outside their authority;
- Michael, Ivory, and ScriptMaker declare income claims outside generated output;
- prohibited concepts never appear in any allowed-output contract; and
- the planned ScriptMaker opportunity exercise accepts only leadership-approved
  context while forbidding qualification, automatic sending, and income projections.

This suite complements the existing behavior-specific ScriptMaker, Ivory,
Michael response-contract, Steve, and outcomes tests. It does not replace them.
